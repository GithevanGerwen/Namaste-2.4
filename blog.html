Namaste brand mission 

At Namaste our mission is to create a connection with our customers and members that is build on trust and support. It is important for us that our members are free to share their problems and experiences and that we will act upon that in a proffesional way. No one should be left out or not feel included. Together with our members we want to create an unique and helpfull experience that will hopefully improve their mental health.
